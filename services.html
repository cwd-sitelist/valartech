Electrical Control Panel Fabrication

At Valar Tech, we design and assemble sophisticated electrical control panels that serve a variety of industrial applications. Our panels are built to comply with the highest standards such as ISA, IEEE, NEMA, IEC, UL, and BS, ensuring reliability, safety, and operational efficiency. Each panel is custom-designed to meet the unique requirements of our clients, incorporating features like real-time monitoring and fault detection systems, which enhance troubleshooting and reduce downtime.

Imports & Exports

At Valar Tech, we offer comprehensive import and export services tailored to the needs of the industrial automation sector. With a strong global network and deep technical expertise, we specialize in sourcing, retrofitting, and supplying automation equipment and components to clients across the world.

Import Capabilities

We assist clients in identifying, sourcing, and importing high-quality industrial machinery, spare parts, and control components from global suppliers. Our services include:

  • Procurement of automation components, including PLCs, HMIs, SCADA systems, servo drives, sensors, and control panels
  • Sourcing rare or obsolete parts required for existing systems
  • Importing spare parts and machinery for preventive and corrective maintenance
  • Buying scrap/used machinery for retrofitting and upgradation to modern standards
  • Handling customs clearance, logistics, and delivery to your facility

Whether you need a specific motor controller, advanced vision system, or heavy-duty machinery from abroad, we take care of everything — from supplier coordination to doorstep delivery.

Export Capabilities

We manufacture and export customized automation solutions tailored to client requirements across various industries. Our export services include:

  • Special Purpose Machines (SPM) for manufacturing and process automation
  • Custom-built control panels for industrial machinery, HVAC, marine, and vehicle applications
  • Retrofitted machinery upgraded with latest automation controls
  • Integrated automation systems for packaging, assembly, and material handling
  • Electrical enclosures, drives, and safety systems for industrial applications

All exported solutions meet international standards, with documentation and technical support for global deployment.

PLC programing

Valar Tech offers expert PLC programming services tailored to enhance machinery operations and integration. HMI / SCADA Design work

We provide state-of-the-art HMI and SCADA design services that facilitate effective interaction between humans and machine systems. Our designs ensure that operators can easily monitor and control complex processes through graphical user interfaces and real-time data acquisition systems. These systems are critical for maintaining high levels of accuracy and efficiency in operations, especially in sectors like healthcare and electronics manufacturing.

Robotic Automation

ICE Engine (ECU – Remapping / Diagnostic / Optimization and Tuning)

At Valar Tech, we are dedicated to enhancing your vehicle's performance through expert Engine Control Module (ECM) tuning and optimization. Our team of skilled technicians specializes in adjusting engine parameters to improve horsepower, torque, and fuel efficiency across a diverse range of vehicles including Indian cars, imported cars, premium automobiles, as well as commercial and agricultural vehicles.

Beyond performance enhancements, we offer comprehensive services such as fault resetting, key programming, odometer correction, and airbag crash resets. We employ the latest in diagnostic techniques to ensure all modifications meet vehicle standards and maintain safety and reliability. Our commitment is to unlock your vehicle’s optimal performance while ensuring it remains efficient and dependable.

For those interested in maximizing their vehicle’s capabilities through professional ECM tuning, Valar Tech is your go-to expert. We provide detailed consultations to tailor our services to your specific needs. Visit us today or contact us to discover how we can elevate your driving experience.
